# Digital Marketing Performance Optimization

## Overview

This project analyzes digital marketing campaign performance to support data-driven decision making for a digital marketing agency managing multiple client accounts across different industries. The analysis focuses on evaluating advertising effectiveness and efficiency by examining key performance metrics and identifying actionable insights to improve revenue and profitability.

## Objectives

* Measure and evaluate digital advertising performance using key metrics such as Click-Through Rate (CTR), Purchase Value (Omzet), and Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS).
* Analyze performance trends based on time, campaign objective (Traffic vs Sales), industry, and client account.
* Identify seasonal patterns and high-performing segments to support optimization strategies.
* Generate business recommendations for budget allocation and campaign improvement.

## Key Analyses

* **Overall Performance**: Evaluation of total CTR, total omzet, and overall ROAS.
* **Campaign Objective Analysis**: Comparison of performance between Traffic and Sales campaigns.
* **Time-Based Analysis**: Identification of performance trends and seasonal patterns across months.
* **Industry and Account Analysis**: Comparison of omzet and ROAS across industries and client accounts to identify high-performing segments.

## Key Insights

* Ads show strong engagement with an overall CTR of around 10 percent.
* Advertising activities contribute significantly to total revenue, generating approximately 10.5 billion in omzet.
* Overall ROAS remains below 1, indicating the need for efficiency improvements.
* Performance shows clear seasonal patterns, with stronger results in April–May and October–November.
* The Fashion industry consistently outperforms Beauty and FMCG in terms of omzet and ROAS.

## Business Recommendations

* Increase budget allocation during high-performing periods to maximize revenue.
* Prioritize and further develop campaigns in the Fashion industry as a primary growth driver.
* Replicate successful strategies from high-ROAS accounts to improve performance in other industries.
* Optimize conversion strategies, including targeting and landing pages, to improve overall ROAS.

## Tools and Technologies

* Jupyter Notebook for analysis workflow

## Output
